## The first 15 minutes

Nobody is graceful at the start. You'll wobble. You'll probably fall in — once, maybe twice. That's fine. That's the point. The e-foil's motor cuts on impact, so the worst outcome is a splash.

The moment most people remember isn't the first flight. It's the second. Because the first time you lift, it's shock. The second time, you choose to do it. That's when the grin starts and doesn't stop.

Most guests are flying consistently within their first hour.

---

## What makes it different from surfing or wakeboarding

With surfing, you're chasing the wave. With wakeboarding, you're chasing the boat. With an e-foil, you're the wave. There's no wake, no noise, no waiting for conditions. Calm water is ideal water.

The silence is the part nobody talks about enough. You're hovering 40cm above the surface, moving at 25km/h, and the only sound is the wind. It's genuinely surreal.

---

## Who it's for

Beginners. Genuinely. We designed every session around the assumption that you've never done this before, because most people haven't. Our instructors get you standing and moving in the first 15 minutes. The rest of the session is yours.

If you've surfed or wakeboarded before, you'll adapt faster — but prior experience isn't a requirement. We've taught 12-year-olds and 68-year-olds. We've taught people who told us they have terrible balance. They flew.
