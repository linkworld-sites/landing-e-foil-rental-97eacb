# Cookie Policy

**Last updated: 2 July 2026**

This Cookie Policy explains how E-Foil Rental ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

---

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember your preferences and understand how you use it.

---

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function. They cannot be disabled.

| Cookie | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| `lw_consent` | Stores your cookie consent preference |
| `lw_funnel_session` | Maintains your session for booking and funnel tracking |

### Analytics Cookies (Optional)

We use anonymised analytics to understand how visitors use our site — which pages are popular, how long people stay, and where they come from. This helps us improve the experience.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose |
|--------|----------|---------|
| Analytics session | LinkWorld | Anonymised usage tracking |

### Marketing Cookies (Optional)

If enabled, these cookies allow us to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose |
|--------|----------|---------|
| `_fbp` | Meta | Measures ad performance on Facebook/Instagram |

---

## Managing Your Preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time:

- **On first visit** — use the consent banner at the bottom of the page.
- **At any time** — clear your browser cookies and revisit the site to see the banner again.
- **Browser settings** — most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking essential cookies may affect site functionality.
