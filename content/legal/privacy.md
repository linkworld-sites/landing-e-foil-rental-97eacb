# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2 July 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how E-Foil Rental ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use our website and services.

---

## What We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

- **Contact details** — name, email address, and phone number when you submit a booking request or contact form.
- **Booking information** — session type, preferred date, and any notes you provide.
- **Usage data** — anonymised information about how you interact with our website (pages visited, session duration) via analytics tools.

We do not collect payment card information directly. Any payment processing is handled by our third-party payment partner.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Respond to booking requests and confirm sessions
- Send pre-session information (what to bring, directions, what to expect)
- Improve our website and services based on usage patterns
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure our website functions correctly, and optional analytics and marketing cookies to understand how visitors use our site. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via our cookie consent banner or by contacting us.

See our [Cookie Policy](/legal/cookies) for details.

---

## Data Retention

We retain booking and contact information for up to 2 years to provide our services and handle any follow-up queries. Analytics data is retained in anonymised form.

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Request access to the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time

To exercise these rights, email us at **info@efoilrental.com**.
